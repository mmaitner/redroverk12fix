§1Overview

Red Rover Kiosk Touch Fix is a small utility extension that improves how the Red Rover kiosk page behaves on touchscreens. It disables accidental text selection and long-press menus and makes taps register reliably, so the kiosk responds cleanly to touch.

The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information. This policy explains exactly what the extension does and does not do with your information.

§2Information we collect

None. The extension collects no personal information, no usage data, and no device information. It does not use cookies, does not include analytics or tracking, and does not contain advertising.

§3How the extension works

The extension runs only on pages under https://kiosk.redroverk12.com/*. On those pages it loads a stylesheet and a small script that adjust how the page responds to touch:

  • Styling (CSS) disables text selection and touch callouts and normalizes scroll and touch behavior.
  • Script (JavaScript) blocks the browser's text-selection and context-menu popups (such as the ChromeOS “Copy | Select all” menu) and, when you lift your finger, triggers the click on the button or element you tapped so the kiosk responds reliably to touch.

Both the stylesheet and the script run entirely inside your browser on that page. They read no personal information and send nothing anywhere.

§4Permissions

The extension requests no special permissions. It only registers a content script scoped to the Red Rover kiosk domain, which is required to apply its touch fixes there. It has no access to any other website, to your browsing history, or to your files.

§5Third-party services

The extension uses no third-party services, servers, or APIs. It shares no data with anyone, because it holds no data to share.

§6Children's privacy

Red Rover kiosks are used in K–12 school settings. The extension collects no information from any user, including children under 13, and is therefore consistent with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar rules. Because nothing is collected, nothing about a child (or any user) is ever gathered, stored, or shared.

§7Changes to this policy

If this extension's behavior ever changes in a way that affects privacy, this page will be updated and the effective date above will be revised. Continued use of the extension after an update means you accept the revised policy.
